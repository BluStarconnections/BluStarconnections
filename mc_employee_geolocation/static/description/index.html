Employee Attendance Geo Location (ODOO 14)

  • With this module you can get location when employee mark attendance.
  • Inside the Employee form view, check mark the field Track Location? if you want to track employee location while marking attendance.


  • After checking above field, you will get location of employee from where they check_in and check_out their attendance.
  • you will get City, Region, Country, Longitude and Latitude in location.
